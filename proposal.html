Openbook's proposed Privacy Controls for Facebook

  1. 1 - Facebook's privacy options are little-used and overcomplicated

    Facebook has a bewildering number of privacy settings.
     
    As Openbook's search service shows, this results in many users sharing private details of their lives with the entire planet, without their knowledge or permission.

  2. 2 - We say: make privacy clear

    Here's how we think Facebook should address this problem: Add a prominent privacy control to everyone's home page.
     
    It's simple and it instantly communicates your privacy settings.

  3. 3 - More detail with just a click

    Clicking the slider shows a quick breakdown of your current privacy settings.

  4. 4 - Dragging to the right makes everything public

    Dragging to the right makes everything public. Dragging to the left makes everything private.
     
    Simple. Just how it should be.

  5. 5 - As fine grained as you need

    Users can still customize their settings in detail.
